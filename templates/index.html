Welcome

Welcome to Canada’s COVID-19 vaccination portal. In an effort to stop the effects of the worldwide coronavirus pandemic - declared by the World Health Organization on March 11, 2020 - we encourage as many Canadians as possible to get vaccinated at no charge.

Science has shown that vaccination is the most effective way to protect your family from COVID-19 and stop the spread of the virus. When a large percentage of the population becomes immune to COVID-19 the spread of the virus will slow down or stop. Every Canadian that gets vaccinated brings our country one step closer to that day.

Vaccines will not be mandatory, however we encourage your household to join our nationwide effort to leave the coronavirus pandemic behind and move forward as a healed nation.

Personal Information

Do you consider yourself to be a high-risk individual? High-risk individuals meet one or more of the following criteria

See Criteria
  • adults in or from Indigenous communities not included in stage 1, including those who live in urban settings
  • residents and staff of all other shared living settings, such as:
    • shelters
    • group homes
    • correctional facilities
    • housing for migrant workers
  • adults 60 to 69 years of age:
    1. beginning with those 65 years of age or older, then
    2. decreasing the age limit to 60 years
  • adults in racialized and marginalized communities
  • first responders, including:
    • police
    • military
    • firefighters
    • coast guard
  • frontline essential workers who can’t work virtually and have direct close physical contact with the public, such as:
    • postal services
    • border services
    • rotational workers
    • grocery store staff
    • transportation workers
    • those involved in the pandemic response
    • food production or manufacturing workers
    • teachers and school or childcare staff not working virtually
  • essential primary caregivers for people who:
    • can’t care for themselves and
    • are at high risk of severe illness from COVID-19 due to advanced age (60 years of age or older)
